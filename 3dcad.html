What is sketching wth Fusion360?

Sketching with Fusion 360 is a process of creating two-dimensional representations of objects and designs using the CAD (Computer-Aided Design) software Fusion 360.

It allows users to visually define the shape and dimensions of their ideas, using tools such as lines, arcs, and splines, which can then be extruded or revolved to create three-dimensional models.

Sketching with Fusion 360 provides a versatile and intuitive platform for designers and engineers to ideate, iterate, and refine their concepts before transitioning into the 3D modeling and prototyping stages of product development.

『 ☆ Build the Lego brick with Fusion 360 ☆ 』

  1. Launch Fusion 360 and create a new design or open an existing project.
  2. Select the "Sketch" workspace from the top toolbar.
  3. Choose the XY plane or any other desired plane where you want to create the Lego brick.
  4. Click on the "Create Sketch" button to start a new sketch on the chosen plane.
  5. Use the sketching tools provided in the toolbar to draw the outline of the Lego brick. Typically, a Lego brick has a rectangular shape with studs on the top. You can use lines, rectangles, and circles to create the desired shape.

  6. Apply dimensions and constraints to define the size and relationships between different sketch elements. Ensure that the dimensions align with the standard Lego brick measurements.

  7. Once your sketch is complete, click on the "Stop Sketch" button to exit the sketching mode.
  8. To give the Lego brick thickness, select the sketch you just created in the browser on the left side of the screen.
  9. Click on the "Create" tab in the toolbar and select the "Extrude" command.
  10. In the dialog box that appears, specify the desired extrusion distance to give the Lego brick its thickness. Again, ensure that the thickness aligns with the standard Lego brick measurements.
  11. Choose the appropriate extrusion options such as solid or surface extrusion.

  12. Click the "OK" button to confirm the extrusion. Fusion 360 will generate the 3D model of the Lego brick by extruding the sketch.
  13. Now, you can add the studs to the top surface of the Lego brick. Create circular sketches for the studs using the circle tool.

  14. Extrude the circular sketches to create the studs by selecting them individually and using the "Extrude" command.

  15. Apply appropriate dimensions and constraints to ensure the studs are accurately positioned and sized.

『 ☆ Build a Mug with Handle In Fusion 360 ☆ 』

    1. Select the "Create" menu from the top toolbar and choose "Cylinder".
    2. Specify the dimensions for the mug (diameter: 50mm, height: 80mm).
    3. Position the mug in the desired location in the workspace.
    4. Select the "Create" menu again and choose "Sketch".
    5. Draw an arc at the base of the mug to create a smooth transition (use the arc tool and specify the desired radius).

    6. Exit the sketch mode and switch to the "Create" menu.
    7. Choose "Extrude" and specify the thickness of the mug (2.5mm).
    8. Select the "Create" menu once more and choose "Sketch".
    9. Draw the handle shape using lines, arcs, or other sketching tools.

    10. Exit the sketch mode and switch to the "Create" menu.
    11. Choose "Extrude" and specify the thickness of the handle (6mm).

    12. Position the handle on the mug and adjust its orientation as needed.

    13. Save your design and export it in the desired file format.
